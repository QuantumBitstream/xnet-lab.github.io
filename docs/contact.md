### Let's Collaborate

Whether you're a start-up scaling your platform, a defence contractor
exploring resilient connectivity, or a healthcare provider seeking
contactless diagnostics — **XIS Lab invites you to co-create the future**.

We are open to:
- 🤝 **Industry partnerships** and sponsored research
- 🎓 **PhD and PostDoc** applications
- 🔬 **Joint grant applications** with academic collaborators
- 📄 **Licensing** of our IP and technology transfer
